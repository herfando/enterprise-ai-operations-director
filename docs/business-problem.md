# Business Problem

## Overview

Modern enterprises generate massive amounts of operational data every day across multiple departments.

However, critical operational decisions are still heavily dependent on manual reporting, disconnected systems, and human-driven analysis.

Enterprise leaders receive information from many departments, but transforming fragmented information into fast and accurate decisions remains a major challenge.

Enterprise AI Operations Director addresses this challenge by transforming enterprise data into autonomous operational intelligence.

---

# Current Enterprise Challenges

## 1. Data Fragmentation Across Departments

Operational data exists across different formats and systems:

- Excel spreadsheets
- CSV files
- PDF reports
- PowerPoint presentations
- Images
- Maintenance logs
- Safety reports
- Business documents
- Text communications

Each department has valuable information, but the data is isolated.

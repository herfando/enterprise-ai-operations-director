# Workflow Design

## Example Scenario

Production Quality Incident Management

---

# Step 1 - Data Collection

AI receives:

- Production report
- QCQA report
- Maintenance history
- SOP document
- Previous incident records

---

# Step 2 - Analysis

AI identifies:

Problem:

Reject rate increased.

Example:

Previous reject:
0.8%

Current reject:
5.2%

---

# Step 3 - Cross Department Reasoning

AI evaluates:

Production:

- Output loss detected

QCQA:

- Defect pattern identified

Maintenance:

- Machine maintenance overdue

Finance:

- Cost impact calculated

PPIC:

- Delivery risk evaluated

---

# Step 4 - AI Decision

Decision:

High priority operational issue.

Root cause probability:

Cooling system instability.

Business impact:

- Production loss
- Delivery risk
- Increased cost

---

# Step 5 - Action Execution

AI executes:

- Generate corrective action plan
- Request approval
- Create maintenance workflow
- Notify responsible teams
- Schedule QC verification

---

# Step 6 - Monitoring

AI monitors:

- Reject improvement
- Machine status
- Production recovery
- Customer delivery impact

---

# Step 7 - Continuous Improvement

Final outcome:

Problem resolved.

AI records:

- Root cause
- Action effectiveness
- Future prevention strategy

---

# Complete Agentic Loop

Observe

↓

Analyze

↓

Decide

↓

Execute

↓

Monitor

↓

Improve
